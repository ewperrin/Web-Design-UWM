Husco is an engineering and manufacturing company headquartered in Waukesha, Wisconsin. Founded in 1985, it specializes in designing and producing hydraulic and electrohydraulic control systems used in a variety of industries, including construction, automotive, and agriculture. The company focuses on innovation, delivering efficient and high-performance products tailored to specific needs in each industry, often providing customized solutions for its clients.
Husco's expertise lies in creating technology that enhances the efficiency, productivity, and safety of machines. Their products, such as hydraulic valves and electronic controls, are used in applications like off-highway vehicles, heavy machinery, and automotive systems, particularly in powertrain and chassis controls.
In addition to its technical contributions, Husco has built a strong reputation for its workplace culture and commitment to community involvement. The company places a high emphasis on employee development, offering various training and mentorship programs to foster innovation and professional growth. Husco is also known for its community engagement and philanthropic efforts, including supporting local education, environmental sustainability, and youth development initiatives.
With a commitment to sustainability and efficiency, Husco continues to push forward with new technologies that meet the demands of modern industries while minimizing environmental impact.
